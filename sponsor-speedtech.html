About Speed Tech Racing

Speed Tech Racing is a world-leading manufacturer of high-performance motorcycle components and racing technology. With over 20 years of experience in elite motorsport, Speed Tech Racing has become synonymous with innovation, precision engineering, and championship-winning performance.

Our partnership with Harrison Dessoy represents the perfect synergy between cutting-edge technology and exceptional athletic talent. Together, we're pushing the boundaries of what's possible in World Superbikes competition.

From advanced aerodynamics to state-of-the-art telemetry systems, Speed Tech Racing provides Harrison with the technological edge needed to compete at the highest level of motorcycle racing.
