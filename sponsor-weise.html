About Weise Clothing

Weise is a leading manufacturer of premium motorcycle clothing and protective gear, dedicated to providing riders with the highest quality equipment for safety, comfort, and performance. With decades of experience in the motorcycle industry, Weise has built a reputation for innovation, durability, and rider-focused design.

From everyday riding gear to high-performance racing equipment, Weise offers a comprehensive range of products designed to meet the needs of riders at every level. Their commitment to quality and safety makes them the perfect partner for Harrison's racing journey.

Weise's product range includes motorcycle jackets, trousers, gloves, boots, and complete protective gear systems, all engineered with advanced materials and cutting-edge safety features to provide maximum protection without compromising on comfort or style.
