About Harrison

Harrison Dessoy is an elite athlete preparing to compete at the highest level of motorcycle racing in 2026. With years of dedication, training, and passion for motorsport, Harrison represents the next generation of world-class racing talent.

From his early days in competitive racing to now preparing for the world stage, Harrison has demonstrated exceptional skill, determination, and sportsmanship. His commitment to excellence and relentless pursuit of perfection make him a formidable competitor in the Superbike arena.

As Harrison embarks on this incredible journey to compete at the highest level of motorcycle racing, he brings with him a unique blend of technical expertise, physical conditioning, and mental fortitude that sets him apart from the competition.
