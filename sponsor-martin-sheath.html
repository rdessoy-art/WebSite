About Martin Sheath & Eastern Garage Racing

Martin Sheath is the owner of Eastern Garage (Essex) Limited, which he took over in 1989. Eastern Garage was originally established in 1950 and has been serving the local community with various car franchises for over 60 years.

With his team of 10 employees, Martin and Eastern Garage serve not only the local community but also fleet commercial operators as far away as Enfield, plus the local Ministry of Defence Police headquarters at Wethersfield.

Eastern Garage represents a blend of traditional automotive excellence and modern service standards, making them an ideal product partner for Harrison's racing journey.
